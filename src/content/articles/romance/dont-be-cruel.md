## Story Overview

Maya Nemugasa is caught cheating on an exam by his classmate Naoki Shiba. Shiba offers a deal: he will not report the cheating in exchange for Maya's compliance with his demands. Maya, without options, agrees.

This is the series' beginning. What the series does with this beginning is its actual content.

Shiba has reasons for wanting Maya's attention that are not fully explained in the early volumes. Maya's compliance is not simple — his feelings about what is happening are more complicated than pure reluctance. As the series develops, both characters' interior positions become clearer, and the initial power dynamic shifts toward something more mutual.

The nine volumes follow this reversal: from coercive beginning to a relationship where both parties have chosen rather than been forced into their positions.

## Characters

**Maya Nemugasa** — A protagonist whose initial passivity is a response to circumstance rather than character; his actual personality and genuine feelings emerge as the series gives him room to express them.

**Naoki Shiba** — A character whose coercive behavior has specific reasons that become clearer; his inability to simply ask for what he wants is the series' central character problem.

## Art Style

Nekota's art is clean and expressive — the school setting is clearly communicated, the character designs are distinctive, and the emotional escalation from forced to genuine is handled with appropriate visual storytelling.

## Cultural Context

Don't Be Cruel ran in Canna from 2011 to 2017. The blackmail/dubious-consent entry point is a recurring structure in boys' love manga; this series represents a competent execution of the pattern with genuine character development beyond the premise.

## What I Love About It

The reversal. The series is aware that the starting point is indefensible and works to move both characters past it. By the end, Shiba must genuinely ask for what he wants and Maya must genuinely answer. The process of getting there is the series' strongest content.
