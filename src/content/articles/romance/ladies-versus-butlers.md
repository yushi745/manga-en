## Story Overview

Hino Akiharu enrolls in Hakureiryou Academy's servant-training course. The school trains both nobles — ladies who will run households and attend events — and servants who will work for them. The two populations interact constantly and in specific hierarchical ways.

Akiharu looks like a delinquent. This is a purely visual misrepresentation — he is not a delinquent — but it causes constant misunderstandings with the ladies' course students who encounter him.

His childhood friend Sernia Flameheart (energetic, aggressive, red-haired twin-tails) has enrolled in the ladies' course and immediately resumes what appears to be a lifetime project of tormenting him. The dignified Flaminia Ede Lort Viewers (calm, aristocratic) has her own interest in Akiharu that gradually becomes apparent.

The harem grows. The fanservice is constant.

## Characters

**Hino Akiharu** — A harem protagonist whose intimidating face is the running gag foundation; beneath the rough exterior he is genuinely earnest.

**Sernia** — The most energetic and consistently present of the harem members; her childhood connection to Akiharu gives her the most developed relationship.

**Flaminia** — The dignified rival whose interest in Akiharu operates at the opposite register from Sernia's.

## Art Style

## Cultural Context

Ladies versus Butlers! ran in Monthly Comic Dragon Age from 2007 to 2014. The butler/maid service school setting draws from a specific subset of Japanese school fantasy that imagines aristocratic service culture as an education system.

## What I Love About It

The Sernia and Flaminia rivalry. The two female leads have completely different personalities and completely different approaches to Akiharu, which keeps the harem format from feeling interchangeable. Their competition is the series' most entertaining content.
