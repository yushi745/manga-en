## Story Overview

Aria has carried a terrible family curse since childhood: any man who falls in love with her transforms into a savage beast. She's watched it destroy her family and has resigned herself to a life of isolation. Then she meets Sakura — a strange young man who seems to be able to resist the curse. But as their connection deepens, Aria realizes that Sakura has his own dark secrets, and that the origin of her curse may be far more ancient and sinister than she knew.

## Characters

The cast of *Night of the Beasts* is built around contrasting personalities that push each other to grow. ## Art Style
