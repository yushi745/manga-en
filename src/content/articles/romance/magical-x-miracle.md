## Story Overview

Merleawe is an ordinary village girl with a talent for healing magic. One day she finds herself summoned to the royal capital and told she must pose as Sylthfarn, the kingdom's beloved Master Wizard who has gone missing. Problem: Sylthfarn is a young man, and Merleawe is most definitely not. With a magical disguise and a crash course in court politics, she must convince an entire kingdom — including Sylthfarn's devoted retainers — that she is who they believe her to be.

## Characters

The cast of *Magical × Miracle* is built around contrasting personalities that push each other to grow. ## Art Style

Yuzu Mizutani's artwork reflects the era and publication it emerged from.
