## Story Overview

Issei Hyodo's dream is a harem. His first date kills him. Rias Gremory, a third-year student who is secretly one of the devil nobility, revives him as her servant using a Bishop piece from the devil chess-based system of servants.

Issei discovers he possesses a Sacred Gear — one of the divine artifacts distributed to humans — called Boosted Gear, which doubles its user's power every ten seconds. This makes him potentially dangerous but also extremely useful. He joins Rias's peerage, which poses as the Occult Research Club, and begins learning what it means to be a devil.

The series follows the escalating conflict involving the fallen angels of Grigori, the remnants of Heaven's forces, and the Underworld's devil factions, with Issei's Boosted Gear becoming increasingly central to the conflict.

## Characters

**Issei Hyodo** — His quality is unusual: he is genuinely motivated by wanting to be strong enough to protect the people he loves, and his stated desire for a harem coexists with sincere affection for specific people. The series develops his feelings with more honesty than the premise implies.

**Rias Gremory** — The devil noble who revived him develops beyond her initial role as the powerful senior. Her specific history and the political pressures she navigates provide the series' larger stakes.

**The peerage** — Asia Argento, Koneko, Akeno, Xenovia, and others are developed across 25 volumes with individual arcs and motivations. The ensemble becomes the series' genuine strength.

## Art Style

Miyama-Zero's art handles the extensive fan service and the action sequences with consistent quality. The Sacred Gear designs are visually distinctive, and the character designs are clear across a large cast. The mythology-inspired designs for the various supernatural factions are inventive.

## Cultural Context

High School DxD filters Christian theology, Islamic angels, and Western supernatural tradition through Japanese fantasy conventions — the result is mythology that is freely adapted rather than faithful and all the more entertaining for it. The chess-based devil servant system is an original invention that provides interesting social structure.

## What I Love About It

The combat sequences where Boosted Gear's mechanics interact with other Sacred Gears or supernatural powers — where the power-doubling creates specific tactical problems and solutions rather than just making Issei generally stronger — are the series' most creative content.

## What English-Speaking Fans Say

Western readers describe High School DxD as the series that turned them from viewers of the anime into readers of the manga — the source material is consistently described as richer than its adaptation. The mythology development is praised by readers who didn't expect to find it interesting.

## Memorable Scene ⚠️ Spoiler Warning

Issei's confrontation with Raiser Phoenix — the antagonist whose regeneration appears to make him unbeatable and whose relationship to Rias provides the series' first major stakes — is the series' first complete action climax and the moment the series earns its premise.
