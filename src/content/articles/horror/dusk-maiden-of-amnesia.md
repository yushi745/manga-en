## Story Overview

Yuuko Kanoe died in the old school building 60 years ago and has haunted it ever since, with no memories of why or how. First-year student Teiichi Niiya, who can see Yuuko, agrees to help investigate. The Paranormal Investigations Club they form attracts two more members. The mystery deepens as Yuuko's memories begin to surface.

## Characters

The cast of *Dusk Maiden of Amnesia* is built around contrasting personalities that force each other to grow.
